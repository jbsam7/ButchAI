8. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information:

  • SQL Protection: Use of parameterized queries to guard against SQL injection attacks.

9. International Data Transfers

Our services are primarily offered within the United States, specifically South Dakota. If we transfer data internationally, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data, such as standard contractual clauses or compliance with relevant data protection regulations.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • Cookie Management: Currently, users do not have direct control over cookie settings on our platform. Cookies are used solely to track active sessions while logged in to provide necessary functionalities like messaging and notifications.

Our services are intended for users who are 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

Note: While minors can use our service, we ensure that their data is handled with the same level of security and privacy as adult users. We do not sell or share data collected from minors with third parties.
